What is mPS?

We analyzed all protein-coding genes for their potential to predict survival, and validated by integrating 37 international breast cancer cohorts (nearly 7000 patients). Machine-learning algorithms known as random-forest classifier and neural network elucidated 23 genes and their weights.

All you need is the binary expression status (high or low) of those 23 genes.
